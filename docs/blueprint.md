## Core Features:

- Central Dashboard: Visualize revenue, expenses, and profit in a single, streamlined view for the environmental sector in Minas Gerais.
- Integrated Calendar: Schedule and view appointments, deadlines, and environmental audits related to business activities.
- Client Information Management: Manage client details, environmental permits, and project-specific data.
- Invoice Generation: Create and send professional invoices for environmental consulting, assessments, and services.
- Sustainability Reporting Tool: AI-powered tool to generate sustainability reports based on project data and environmental metrics. The tool can reason about which metrics apply to the particular situation.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep green (#388E3C) to represent growth, sustainability, and the environment of Minas Gerais.
- Background color: Off-white (#F5F5F5) provides a clean and professional backdrop.
- Accent color: Light green (#81C784) for call-to-action buttons and highlights, promoting an eco-friendly feel.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for a modern and readable appearance.
- Use eco-friendly and business-related icons throughout the application to enhance usability and visual appeal.
- Maintain a clean, organized layout that prioritizes key metrics and essential functions on the dashboard.
- Use subtle transitions and animations to provide feedback and enhance user experience, keeping it professional and unobtrusive.
